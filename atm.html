How to Withdraw Cash

Follow these simple steps:

1. Insert your card & Enter PIN

show more...

Carefully insert your ATM card into the designated slot. Then, enter your four-digit Personal Identification Number (PIN) using the keypad.

2. Select "Withdrawal"

show more...

On the main menu, choose the "Withdrawal" option to proceed with your cash transaction.

3. Choose or Enter Amount

show more...

Select a pre-defined amount or enter a custom amount you wish to withdraw. Ensure you have sufficient balance.

4. Collect Cash and Card

show more...

Once the transaction is complete, collect your cash from the dispenser and remember to take your card. A receipt will be printed.
